## 💰 Payout Structure
We use a cumulative payout structure based on verified views per post:

- 40K Views → $10
- 200K Views → $20 total
- 500K Views → $50 total

#### Payment Details:

Payments are made monthly via PayPal (strongly preferred).
Cash App is available as a backup, but it’s less reliable and payments can be delayed.
You will be paid as long as your posts follow the campaign guidelines and reach the required view thresholds.
## 📋 Campaign Guidelines & Important Info

To ensure the success of the campaign and your freelancers' efforts, please adhere to the following guidelines:

*   **Authenticity is Key:** Do not sound like a corporate advertisement. Native, story-driven posts perform best on Reddit. Share genuine use cases, personal success stories, or helpful productivity tips that naturally incorporate Upshift.
*   **No Spamming:** Do not mass-post the same promotional text across dozens of subreddits. This will get accounts banned and harm the brand's reputation.
*   **Focus on Value:** Posts should provide value to the reader even if they don't click the link. Offer actionable advice or share a relatable struggle.
